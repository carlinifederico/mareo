What is MAREO?

A desktop app that gives you everything you need to plan, track, and deliver projects — without the complexity of enterprise tools.

Built for makers, not managers

MAREO is a lightweight, offline-first project planner designed for freelancers, small teams, and anyone who wants to stay organized without signing up for yet another SaaS.

Your data lives on your machine. No cloud sync, no accounts, no subscriptions required for the core experience.

  • Works offline — no internet required
  • Portable — runs from any folder
  • Fast — vanilla HTML/CSS/JS, no frameworks
  • Private — your data never leaves your device
App size < 2 MB
Dependencies Zero
Startup time Instant
Data stored Locally

Everything you need to plan

📋

Board View

Kanban boards to organize tasks by status. Move cards between columns as work progresses.

Notes

Rich-text notes attached to each project. Keep decisions, ideas, and context in one place.

Expense Tracking
